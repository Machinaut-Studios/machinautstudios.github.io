WHAT IT DOES

We could be coy about it, like we usually are. But Tooter is refreshingly literal: it records the sound, gives it a score, and keeps it forever. Here is the whole machine.

  • FEATURE

    ONE-TAP CAPTURE

    Hit record and watch the live waveform roll. Every performance immortalized in crisp, high-fidelity audio.

  • FEATURE

    THE COURIC SCALE™

    Our patent-pending scoring engine rates each entry on duration, intensity, and raw artistry. Objectively. Ruthlessly.

  • FEATURE

    SEARCHABLE ARCHIVE

    Tag, browse, and replay your entire back catalog. The all-time greats are enshrined in the Hall of Fame.

  • FEATURE

    ONE-TAP SHARING

    Send a certified legend to a friend in seconds. Or keep it for yourself. The archive holds no grudges.

  • FEATURE

    STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE

    No accounts. No uploads. No cloud. What happens in the booth stays in the booth — and on your phone.

  • FEATURE

    HALL OF FAME

    The highest Couric Scale™ scores rise to the top, automatically, forever. Greatness is recognized.
